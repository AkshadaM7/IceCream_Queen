ABOUT US

Founded on October 10, 2020, by Ms. Aakanksha Bapat, IceCream Queen is dedicated to offering a healthier and superior ice cream experience. The company crafts a diverse range of flavors using only the finest natural ingredients, ensuring each product is free from added sugar, synthetic colors, artificial flavors, preservatives, and synthetic additives. IceCream Queen consistently delivers a pure and delicious treat, meeting the demand for indulgent yet guilt-free desserts.
